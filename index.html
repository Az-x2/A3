In regards to population; as of 2023, Australia’s population is sitting at 26 million however there has been discussion from the government that by 2050, Australia would reach 36 million.

This comes from the Treasury’s 2010 Intergenerational Report. However Australia does have some ‘growing pains’ as Sydney and Melbourne constantly expands with each year and is only increasing the cost of services.

The Net Zero plan is Australia's version to cut greenhouse gasses emissions. The main problem with this plan is wondering if the government will actually do anything about it. Running farms on solar energy, switching to electric cars and a more safer and cleaner way to extract from mines would be ideal but judging from what we know, can we trust the government?
